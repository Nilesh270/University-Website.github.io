Facilities

GROUND

The ground has lush green outfield of international standards with proffesional equipments.Along with football goal post and athletics track.

LIBRARY

The library can accommodate 200 students at a time.The library has 40,000 books which cater the requirement of the students of all streams.

CAFE

Our spacious canteen can accomodate around 100 students at a time.It provide different types of cuisine and authentic food to the students.

COMPUTER LAB

The college have 200 advance computers with intel i7 processor installed in the lab.It is well-equipped hub of e-learning where students can explore and evolve.

OPEN AMPHITHEATER

We have a large Open Amphitheater in the campus.All the college Fest and Events are organised there.It can occupy 1000 students at a time.

HOSTEL

There are 2 different hostel for Boys & Girls.The hostel can accommodate 1200 students.The mess of the Hostel provides good & hygienic food to the students.
