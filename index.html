About Us

Eduford University was founded in 2010 by Shri.Nilesh Balotiya. His dream is to provide Quality Education to the youth of our Country. It was started with just 5 classroom and 200 students.But Today,it proudly Boast of having 4 acres campus consisting of 3 buildings.It has over 2000 teaching and Non-teaching staff,and morethan 10000 students who pass through its hallowed portal each year.
Eduford University is autonomous university and follows the rules and regulationlaid down by the Government for admission.It is accredited as "A++" Grade by NAAC.The admission in the University are purely on the Merit Basis.
