About us

Eduford University was founded in 2010 by Shri.Nilesh Balotiya- a philanthropist and a visionary, envisaged the blending of value education along with the advanced science teaching which eventually gave birth to ‘Eduford University’.

In the following decade, the Eduford University campus grew to 10 institutions catering to the needs of students of undergraduate level. The educational conglomerate located in a lush green campus admeasuring 4 acres of land at Chembur , in the eastern suburb of Mumbai, is an institution to be reckoned with.Eduford University is recognized both regionally & nationally . Shri Nilesh Balotiya and the President of Eduford University has provided a dynamic leadership to this establishment.

In year 2013 and 2014, the college was shortlisted amongst 350 urban colleges& Universities for the ‘Best Institute Award’ and in year 2016, it received “The Best Institute Award” by the Government. Even in year 2020 the university is again shortlisted for the title.
Eduford University was also ranked among TOP TEN science colleges of the city by INDIA TODAY with rank 4 in year 2014, rank 3 in year 2015 & 2016 and rank 7 in year 2017. In a country wide ranking it was ranked 24 th among the best science colleges.
